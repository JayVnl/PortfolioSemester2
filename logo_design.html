Logo Design

My group member Jordy and me both made a logo for Aisha.

He made the 2 logos on the left and i made the 2 logos on the right.
After doing some brand tests on our logo's to Aisha's target audience and input from Aisha herself we both made iterations and ended up with a final result.

The 4th logo was the final product we chose for Aisha, this logo was the favourite of Aisha's target audience and the one she also liked the most.

I learned from this experience that it's always useful and necessary to ask for as much feedback as possible and make iterations according to that.
