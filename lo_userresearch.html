Interview with Simon Peters

Gender: Male

Interviewer: How old are you?
Interviewee: I am 20 years old.

Interviewer: What level of education did you do?
Interviewee: I have finished HAVO and am now studying HBO.

Interviewer: Which genre of music do you mainly listen to?
Interviewee: I mainly listen to cloudrap, experimental R&B and emorap.

Interviewer: Do you ever attend concerts? if so how often?
Interviewee: No never.

Interviewer: Do you use music streaming services such as spotify or soundcloud, and if so how often?
Interviewee: I use spotify every day and soundcloud only for specific artists who don't upload to spotify.

Interviewer: Do you use social media such as facebook / twitter / youtube / snapchat etc, if so how often and why?
Interviewee: Yes, I mainly use instagram and reddit, about an hour and a half a day. I mainly use Instagram for memes but also to follow celebrities and see day to day activities. I use reddit for news and discussions

Interviewer: When do you listen to music and does the type of music change during other activities?
Interviewee: The music that I listen to often stays the same with cooking or training, but when relaxing or a more intimate atmosphere, the music will be a little more chill.

Interviewer: Do you ever visit an artist's website and if so how often and what do you use it for?
Interviewee: Yes, but only if there is something of exclusive merchandise that appeals to me.

Interviewer: How do you discover new music and / or artists?
Interviewee: Suggestions from friends or via youtube or spotify.

Interviewer: Do you listen to Dutch-language music? If so, what do you like about it, if not, why not?
Interviewee: No, actually only English, not many Dutch artists appeal to me.

Feedback

The tone in which I asked questions was good and the interview went smoothly. For some questions, I could have asked a little bit further to obtain more information.

Reflection

So what have i learned from the interviews i have taken?
I should try to loosen up the questions a bit more, i am a very strict person that likes when things are as they should be which makes it easy for me to ask the questions that i have written down but i should focus on trying to follow-up a question depending on the answer i get from the interviewee.

I basically need to receive as much information from a interviewee as i can by constructing well made questions that make it easy for me to come up with a follow-up.

Test Moodboard

The keywords we use in the test:
Cold, Warm, Cheerful, Confident, Curious, Depressed, Enthusiastic, Happy, Modern, Approachable, Insecure, Joyful, Loving, Naughty, Obsessive, Optimistic, Proud, Prickly, Sociable.

From our results we made a top 3 picked keywords and these are:
- Happy
- Warm
- Loving

The goal of the test:
Finding out what kind of feeling our moodboard gives to Aisha's target audience.

Our test users:
Our test users are people that we based on our created persona, this includes primarily women around an age of 16 to 24, in the city of Eindhoven.

Our Test method:
We will be applying the method keyword matching, the tester will have to fill in 3 different dots linked to a keyword they find fitting for the moodboard.

Describing the product or design that we want to test:
We want to test our moodboard that we created for Aisha's audience.

Test Business card colour

The results we got from this test were quite unanymous.
Both Aisha and her target audience found the pink version the most fitting/appealing to Aisha.

The goal of the test:
Finding out which colour business card appeals most to Aisha and her target audience.

Our test users:
Our test users are people that we based on our created persona, this includes primarily women around an age of 16 to 24, in the city of Eindhoven and Aisha herself.

Our Test method:
We will be using a hexa test, the tester can choose one of the 6 coloured business cards as their favourite.

Describing the product or design that we want to test:
We want to test the designs/iterations we made for the favourite business card in the previous business card brand test. (not included in my portfolio).

Reflection

From making and using these tests i have learned how to setup a specific test for anything that i need feedback for.
I will definitely keep on using this method since it is very useful for any future projects i will be making.
