Urban Safari

The day began with a quick tutorial about how to use some of the features of the camera that we got. After this we adventured into the city in a group of 2 while making pictures of buildings/architectures that we thought looked interesting.
Back at school we had another tutorial, this time it was about how to edit our photos.

I found this an interesting assignment that gave me some experience and knowledge about how to use the different features of a camera, how different viewpoints can make a big difference and how to edit photographs using lightroom or photoshop.
I can use these techniques later on whenever i need to make original photographs for like a website or something else.
